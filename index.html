About Me

You've found the online home of Doug Hoepker, an editor, writer, and project manager who has worked in print (newspaper & books), web, and academic publishing. I’m also currently cutting my teeth in front-end web development. This website was hand-coded and built by me.

I live in Baltimore City with my wife, our black cat, and more than 6,000 vinyl records. In my spare time, I enjoy exploring the city of Baltimore and advocating on its behalf, DJ-ing in bars, curating popular music podcast that has thousands of followers, watching the O's, and searching for the next great meal in Baltimore's flourishing food scene.
